Our Story

S Kota's Hotel Muntaj, a well-known establishment in Srungavarapu Kota, was founded by individuals who built the legacy of the hotel. The hotel's founders are acknowledged and thanked for their vision, which led to the establishment of the family-run business. The current owners are proud to continue the legacy and work hard to keep the founders' memory alive.

Muntaj hotel was founded by Sheik Mahaboob Basha and Sheik Mahoobhi in 1974. It's due to their hard work, struggle and our customer's support that has made Muntaj what it is today.

We pride ourselves on creating a warm, welcoming atmosphere where guests can enjoy the vibrant flavors and rich culinary heritage of South India. Every meal tells a story of tradition, culture, and passion for excellent food.
